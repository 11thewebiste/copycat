HYDRA TOWN ROLEPLAY RULES AND REGULATION

This server is mainly focus on serious rp and we expect serious rp from your side and we strictly believe in character progression and interaction. Kindly read the below mentioned rules to help you with your time at HTRP.

1. General Behavior


1.1 You are not allowed to talk upon the religious, political, or sensitive off topic discussion.

1.2 This Server is a Streamer Friendly Server that means no ERP (Erotic Roleplay) or Roleplaying a nude character is allowed.

1.3 This server is not an 18+ server so if anyone reports you for your swearing you will be banned.

1.4 You are not allowed to have roleplay which can deem to have racist, slavery or homophiblic acts.

1.5 You are expected to follow the discord rules and you need to behave properly with everyone. If you have a problem with anyone you report him/her to the admin.

1.6 Disrespecting the Staff or the Server will result in PERMANENT BAN.

2. Account Responsibility and Name


2.1 You will be responsible for your account. If we find that you are sharing your account with another player to play on the server then your account will be PERMANENTLY BANNED. We have AI system to detect if you've shared your account with someone or not.

2.2 Make sure that your Account Username doesn’t Have

   2.2.A Racist or Offensive names

   2.2.B Any kind of promotion or advertisement

   2.2.C Special Characters or Foreign Characters

3. Roleplay Rules


3.1 Random Deathmatch (RDM) – You are not allowed to kill anyone without initiating any kind of the roleplay

3.2 Vehicle Deathmatch (VDM) – You are not allowed to kill anyone by using your vehicle as a weapon or without initiating any kind of roleplay with him.

3.3 Realistic Behavior – You must always behave in a realistic manner and must always be in your character.

3.4 Meta-Gaming – You are not allowed to use Outside Character to benefit In-Character. You are also not allowed to use any other mode communication which is not associated with the server.

3.5 Power Gaming and Fail RP– You are not allowed to abuse the in-game mechanic to give an advantage over the other players.

   3.5.A You are also not allowed to talk when you die.

   3.5.B Misusing /me and /ooc commands

   3.5.C You are not allowed to enter your house whenever a police officer is chasing you.

   3.5.D You are only allowed to change clothes from a clothes shop when in pursuit with a police officer.

3.6 Cop and Ems Baiting – You are not allowed to bait any of the government officials or forcefully try to engage with them.

3.7 Impersonating Players – You are not allowed to impersonate any of the government members.

3.8 New Life Rule – If you take respawn, then you are not allowed to recall the situation which occurred with your character which led to you being downed.

3.9 Character Mixing – At no circumstances, you can mix information/money with your characters.

3.10 Combat Logging – Until and unless the situation you are involved is over you aren’t allowed to leave the server.

3.11 Money-Exploit – Money exploit of any kind will result in permanent ban.

4. Safe Zones


4.1 There are two safe zones in the server

   4.1.A Pillbox

   4.1.B MRPD

4.2 You are not allowed to exit the city from the safe zone when you are in an active situation.

5. Reporting a Player and Banning/Unbanning a Player


5.1 When you are reporting a player make sure you have ample amount of evidence to support your statement.

5.2 We also need the ID of the person who is reporting.

5.3 When you are banned, you need to fill the ban-appeal form and wait for the response.

5.4 If you have any issues with any of the staff members you can fill the Staff-Grievance form.

6. Criminal Activity


6.1 Maximum number of players allowed in any criminal activity is 5. (e.g., Processing Drugs, Gang Meetings, Fights between Groups, etc., Any Criminal Activity)

6.2 Giving out information on the radio will also count you in as a part of that scenario.

6.3 You are not allowed to engage in a combat situation which involves animation. (e.g., You cannot fight where player needs to press E to go inside)

6.4 You are not allowed to use your friends or gang members as hostages. Hostages must be players that have no idea what you are planning.

6.5 You cannot start shooting at police just for attempting to pull you over. Such actions will be classed as clear RDM.

6.6 You cannot start shooting at police just for arresting you for a simple house robbery. Such actions will be classified as clear RDM.

6.7 You cannot just go to an unknown person for the means of Robbing and point a gun at him. Doing So will lead to a Permanent ban without Prior warning.

6.8 Bank Robbery – you must equip a backpack in your outfit always.

 6.8.A Pacific Bank

   6.8.A.1 Minimum of 4 players and Maximum of 6 players will be allowed in this situation

   6.8.A.2 Maximum number of escaping vehicles allowed are 2.

   6.8.A.3 Minimum number of hostages required are 2.

 6.8.B Jewelry/Normal Banks

   6.8.B.1 Maximum number of players allowed in this situation are 5.

   6.8.B.2 Maximum number of escaping vehicles allowed will be 1.

   6.8.B.3 Minimum number of hostages required are 1.

 6.8.C Store Robbery

   6.8.C.1 Maximum number of players allowed in this situation are 2

   6.8.C.2 Maximum number of escaping vehicles allowed will be 1.

   6.8.C.3 Minimum number of hostages required are 1.

7. Staff Rules


7.1 Misusing your in-game powers will result in direct removal.

7.2 As a staff you are not allowed to leak out any of the internal talks or insider information out to the public.

7.3 No favoritism to any of your friends, you must treat everyone equally.
